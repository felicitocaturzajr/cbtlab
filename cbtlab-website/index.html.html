The CBT Solution

One theory.
Infinite clarity.

Candle Break Theory is built on one fundamental truth: price tells you everything. In a bullish market, price breaks the high of the previous candle without breaking its low. In a bearish market, it does the opposite.

That's the foundation. From that single principle, you get structure, momentum, and execution clarity — all without cluttering your chart with lagging indicators.
